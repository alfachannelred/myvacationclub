From the strong sense of environmental protection and eco-conservation, the first project started in 1998 at Edakkal hermitage. Reviving the sub-tropical forest was the first moto along with eco-tourism to sustain. In 20 decades we have revived the local forestation up to more than 50% and now home to a variety of feathers and furs including the giant Malabar squirrel, the Malabar civet cat, and the hornbills. From the foothill of Edakkal caves, a heritage beyond 6000 years, we stepped out to the social needs of the region and established the Hill District Club as a platform for the elite of Wayanad. With a concept of “culture meets nature” we went beyond the spectrum of entitling our privileged members with prestigious clubs pan India and abroad. To add the abode of gods we wandered out to the valleys and lakes of blue lotuses and created Vistara By The Lake and wanderlust has par amounted us to form The Vacation Club.
